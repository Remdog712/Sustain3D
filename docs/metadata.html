You are on: Metadata Next: Storage

Goal

Capture enough descriptive and process context to support interpretation, reuse, and stewardship by documenting identifiers, workflow decisions, rights, storage location, and publication status.

1) How metadata is edited during workflow

  • At setup: object ID, project title, rights baseline, and access level.
  • After capture: devices, settings, operator, and source files.
  • During processing: software versions, major edits, and preservation master path.
  • After derivatives: access formats, print files (if present), and publication notes.
  • During storage/publication: storage location and DOI status/value.

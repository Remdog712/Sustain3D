2) master_inventory.csv headers (minimum)

object_id,title,collection,project,creator,dates,capture_method,source_files,preservation_master,access_derivatives,software_versions,rights,access_level,storage_location,checksum_manifest,notes

“Access level” can be: public / internal / restricted. Keep it explicit.

3) Naming convention (recommended pattern)

[ObjectID]_[Method]_[YYYYMMDD]_v[##]_[stage].[ext]
Example:
PAOAN_LiDAR_20240215_v01_raw.e57
PAOAN_mesh_20240220_v03_pres.obj
PAOAN_web_20240220_v01_access.glb

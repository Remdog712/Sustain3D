Goal

Create a short policy document that defines access, rights, reuse conditions, sensitivity considerations, and review responsibilities before publication.

1) Core policy decisions

  • Access level: public, internal, or restricted.
  • Rights statement: legal status and ownership condition for the model package.
  • Reuse terms: what users may and may not do.
  • Sensitivity notes: any community, ethical, or institutional constraints.

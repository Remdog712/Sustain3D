Glossary

3D model: a digital representation of form, often with textures and materials.
Repository: organized storage and management space for files and records.
Metadata: descriptive and technical information about files and objects.
Paradata: record of processing choices and assumptions.
Preservation master: highest quality retained version for long-term stewardship.
Access derivative: delivery copy made for public or classroom use.
DOI: persistent identifier used for stable publication and citation.
